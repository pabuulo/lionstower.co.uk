Frequently Asked Questions

Personalized Book Submission Reviews are available for BookClub members click here

Does LionsTower Charge Any Fees?

No. LionsTower does not charge any fees to consider submissions or publish any materials. Every publishing expense is covered entirely by LionsTower Ltd. (including editing, cover design, and marketing).

 

How Long Until I Receive a Submissions Response?

We aim to respond to all proposals within a month, but there may be a longer wait at busy times.

 

Can I Submit An Appeal Upon Rejection?

You are always welcomed to re-submit your proposal every six months at a maximum of 3 submissions per Book/Series. If you wish to have your proposal revisited outside the above-outlined conditions, in-house fees may be applied.

 

Can I Pay LionsTower to Publish My Book?

Authors and Companies seek Vanity Press services for several reasons. For example, the necessity of publishing under a short time frame or simply waiving the submission/consideration step.

LionsTower does not charge authors to publish their work unless under Vanity Press Proposal. And this service does not require minimum authors copies purchase. If you are interest in paying for the publication of your book please reach out to lionstower@publicist.com
